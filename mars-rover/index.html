Curiosity-Driven Exploration

NASA’s Mars Exploration Program is a science-driven effort to understand whether Mars was, is, or can be a habitable world. By sending robotic explorers like Curiosity and Perseverance, the Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) seeks to uncover the secrets of the Red Planet’s climate, geology, and potential for ancient life.

Perseverance

The newest resident of Jezero Crater, searching for signs of ancient microbial life.

Curiosity

A car-sized laboratory that has been exploring Gale Crater since 2012.
