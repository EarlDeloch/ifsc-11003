Mission Timelines

Since the 1990s, NASA has successfully landed multiple robotic explorers on Mars. Each mission represents a massive leap in engineering and coordination between Earth and the Red Planet.

The Pathfinder Mission

The Sojourner rover was delivered to the Martian surface via a tetrahedral lander wrapped in airbags. It was the first time NASA used this "bounce to a stop" landing method, which allowed for a safe arrival in the rocky Ares Vallis region.

The Skycrane Maneuver

Because Curiosity was too heavy for airbags, NASA engineered the "Skycrane." A rocket-powered descent stage lowered the rover onto the floor of Gale Crater using nylon tethers, a feat of engineering often referred to as 'Seven Minutes of Terror.'
